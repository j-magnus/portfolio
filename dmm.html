Digital Mark Making

I wanted to focus on what capabilities I had on the computer as a project of exploration. Mark making has always been my beginning method of discovering the boundaries, abilities, and nuances of a medium. Digitally, there are not often opportunities to explore what is possible through rudimentary trial and error.

This exploration began by taking multiple photos of a painting. The painting was moved, zoomed into and out of, and rotated in different shots. Using the sequence of images, a stop motion was then made. This basic stop motion was then manipulated four different times. Multiple forms of computer software was used to alter the video.
