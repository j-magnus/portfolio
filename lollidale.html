Lollidale

Lollidale is a startup from Minneapolis that I did research and strategy for through Design U. Me and my team analyzed the organization and its current position, and then suggested growth opportunities.

Our team found that consistent branding, dynamic content, and social media revitalization were the largest areas for improvement. Below is some content I personally generated for our final deliverable to the client.


Social media posts

Lollidale frequently posted inspirational or funny quotes as part of their scheduled feed. The quotes had low engagement and reach, so we advised using content that had strong brand representation, visual interest, and consistency.
